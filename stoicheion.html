Stoicheion



Click a shape to select it, click again to unselect.

Click on where you want to place shapes on the grid.

Arrange shapes to fill up the grid completely.

You can overlap the same elements.

The more you overlap the greater your score!


[New Shape] replaces the currently selected shape.

It is free to [Rotate] and [Mirror] the selected shape.


You gain a New Shape if you complete the board.
